--- Title

R advanced: programming and packaging

--- Summary

Learn tools and best practice to program and package with the R language.

--- Details

<p>
	This session is directed at intermediate to advanced R users wanting to learn about packaging and web app development.
</p>
<p>
	In this session, you will learn about:
</p>
<ul>
	<li>creating new custom functions</li>
	<li>creating a package to share your functions</li>
	<li>properly documenting your code</li>
	<li>creating a web application with Shiny</li>
	<li>ways to publish a Shiny app</li>
	<li>using RStudio features that make life easier</li>
</ul>
<p>
	 	The software is available on the training room's computers. If you   prefer to use your own laptop, you are also welcome to bring it in (but   please install the software beforehand, or come 15 minutes earlier if   you haven't).
</p>
