--- Title

R advanced: packaging and sharing functions

--- Summary

Learn tools and best practice to create, package and share your custom R functions.

--- Details

<p>
	The strength of the R programming language lies in the endless possibilities custom functions can bring, and a community of users who happily share their contributions with peers.
</p>
<p>
	This session is directed at intermediate to advanced R users wanting to learn about creating, packaging and sharing functions.
</p>
<p>
	In this session, you will learn about:
</p>
<ul>
	<li>creating new custom functions</li>
	<li>packaging your functions into a cohesive collection</li>
	<li>properly documenting your code</li>
	<li>versioning your code</li>
	<li>using online services to share your package</li>
	<li>using RStudio features that make life easier</li>
</ul>
