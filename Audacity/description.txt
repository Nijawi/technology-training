--- Title

Audacity: introduction to audio editing

--- Summary

Learn how to import, slice, clean and optimise audio recordings with professional-grade software.

--- Details

<p>
	Audacity is an open source, cross-platform tool for editing audio files.
</p>
<p>
	This training covers how to:
</p>
<ul>
	<li>import audio into Audacity</li>
	<li>move and delete sections</li>
	<li>clean up background noise</li>
	<li>switch between sound visualisation tools</li>
	<li>use a few essential effects</li>
	<li>export to a variety of formats</li>
	<li>publish audio online</li>
</ul>
<p>
	The software is available on the training computers. However, if you prefer to use your own laptop, please install the software beforehand:
</p>
<p>
	<a href="https://www.audacityteam.org/download/">https://www.audacityteam.org/download/</a>
</p>
<p>
	Please bring your own personal headset if you would like to listen to your project (training computers support both USB and 3.5 mm jack headsets).
</p>
